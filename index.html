Hello:

My name is Alex Merkel and I'm very excited to be here. I've spent the last few years figuring out what I want to do and have FINALLY figured it out. I'm thrilled to have found and be part of the Coding Temple community and look forward to our time together.

Journey:

I spent 3 years at my local university trying to figure out what path I wanted to take. I started off in Computer Engineering, switched over to Statistics, then finally business with an emphasis in finance. Unfortunatley, I needed more time to determine what path I wanted. After a few years in the working world, I started to see what I wanted to do. The trick now was, how do I get there? Those responsibilities add up quick and I needed to find the quickest/best path into coding. After a couple years of planning (probably a bit of avoiding the big leap) I made the decision that it was time. It was clear that a bootcamp would be my best route. The next challenge was, which one? That's when I found Coding Temple. I saw the flexibility, the languages I wanted to, and the reputation. I knew it was the right place and FINALLY took the leap. I'm pursuing this full time and so far love what we're learning. I can't wait to keep learning more and build my future! I also want to say THANK YOU! I have a renewed sense of purpose and know I'm in good hands to get my dreams moving forward.
